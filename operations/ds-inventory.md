## Driver Station Inventory

The following inventory should be maintained exclusively for use with the driver station. 
Anything taken out of the driver station bag should go back into the driver station bag before the end of the night or event.

### Inventory List

* Driver Station PC
* Charger for Driver Station PC
* Power Strip
* 3 controllers
* 2 USB->Ethernet adapters
* 2 Ethernet pigtails
* Robot Tether (long ethernet cable)
